Activities

See the Komodo Dragons

Meet the world's largest reptiles, the Komodo Dragons, in their natural habitat. This tour provides a rare opportunity to see these magnificent creatures on Komodo Island, while learning about their habits and unique ecosystem.

Snorkeling and Diving

Enjoy the stunning underwater beauty of Komodo Island through snorkeling, swimming, or diving. Discover coral reefs rich in biodiversity and various species of colorful fish in crystal clear waters.

Hiking and Exploring

Explore the natural beauty of Komodo Island by hiking the many stunning trekking trails. Witness the beauty of dramatic landscapes, diverse flora and mesmerizing marine panoramas as you explore these beautiful islands.

Boat Tour and Sunset

Join an amazing boat tour to explore the Komodo archipelago. Enjoy a relaxing experience watching the spectacular sunset on the horizon, creating a magical and unforgettable sight.
