Where To Stay

Relax and unwind in the perfect spot that fits your style and budget.

Accomodation

Bintang Flores Hotel

The Bintang Flores Hotel is a five-storey hotel set in a spacious tropical garden. Drawing on Indonesian architectural influences, The Bintang Flores Hotel combines local style and charm with modern conveniences and international standards of service.

Golo Hilltop Hotel

Golo Hilltop is a small Boutique Style hotel run by two Dutch women and presents a pleasant family atmosphere.

Kasuwari Hotel

Kasuwari Hotel is located with easy access to famous tourist destinations in Labuan Bajo. You can easily visit the mesmerizing Pink Beach, the stunning Padar Island, or the Komodo National Park which offers incredible natural beauty. The hotel also provides an airport shuttle service to make your trip easier.

Sylvia Hotel & Resort Komodo

Located on Waecicu Beach in Labuan Bajo, Sylvia Resort Komodo offers a beachfront swimming pool as well as regular speed boat trips to Rinca Island and Komodo National Park. You can also enjoy various types of water activities at this resort.
