Where To Eat

Savor fresh, local flavors while enjoying stunning views that make every meal memorable.

Restaurants

Atlantis Restorant

This restaurant specializes in Indonesian cuisine, particularly Minangkabau dishes, which are known for their rich flavors and use of spices. Visitors often highlight the authenticity of the local dishes and the warm atmosphere. The restaurant is a popular choice for both locals and tourists who want to experience traditional West Sumatran food.

Le Bajo

It's a popular spot for both locals and tourists, known for its great food, relaxing ambiance, and friendly service. Many visitors enjoy the fresh seafood and pasta, with the outdoor seating area offering beautiful views, making it a perfect place to unwind.

Escape Bajo

Casual restaurant known for its cozy ambiance and a mix of Indonesian and Western dishes. It's popular for its stunning sunset views and laid-back vibe, making it a great spot to relax after a day of exploration. Many visitors enjoy the quality of the food and the friendly staff, with dishes like pizza and seafood being frequently recommended.

Senja Eatery

The restaurant emphasizes fresh ingredients, particularly seafood, and is popular for its beautiful sunset views. Guests often commend the friendly service and relaxed vibe, making it an ideal spot for enjoying a meal after a day of exploring.

Coffee Shops

Cafe Melinjo

This café presents a relaxed atmosphere, suitable for relaxing with a beautiful view while enjoying healthy food and refreshing drinks. This place is known for its friendly service and warm atmosphere, becoming a favorite of tourists and locals alike.

Mimamori Bajo

A restaurant with a concept that combines Japanese flavors with local touches, offering a unique dining experience in Labuan Bajo. It is known for its serene atmosphere, modern interior, and fresh sushi and seafood dishes.

Molas Cafe

Casual cafe known for its cozy ambiance and a mix of Indonesian and Western dishes. It's popular for its stunning sunset views and laid-back vibe, making it a great spot to relax after a day of exploration. Many visitors enjoy the quality of the food and the friendly staff, with dishes like pizza and seafood being frequently recommended.

Casa Selini Cafe

Well-known for offering a mix of Greek and Indonesian cuisine in a cozy setting. Guests enjoy its fresh seafood, salads, and vegetarian-friendly options. The cafe has an inviting atmosphere, with good reviews mentioning both the food and service.
